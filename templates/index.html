Agikuyu Marriage Traditions

The Kikuyu people have rich marriage customs that include:

  • Kuhanda Ithigi - The planting of the branch ceremony, symbolizing the union of two families
  • Ruracio - The traditional dowry ceremony where families formally unite
  • Ngurario - The actual wedding ceremony with traditional vows
Iteso Marriage Traditions

The Iteso community celebrates marriage through:

  • Aitodiar - The formal introduction ceremony between families
  • Akutet - The bride price negotiation and agreement
  • Akuuta - The traditional wedding celebration with dancing and feasting

Both traditions emphasize the importance of family unity, respect for elders, and the celebration of love through community gathering.
